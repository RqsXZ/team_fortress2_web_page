What is Team Fortress 2?

TF2 is, as said above, a multiplayer first-person shooter game developed by Valve. It started as a sequence to a mod for Quake, which was released in 1996. This game is a bit old, being first released back in 2007. (Despite its age, my potato computer still can't run it, but that's a different story). TF2 is as chaotic as this page's HTML and CSS code, but still a lot of fun. It is a team-based game with 9 available characters to choose from. So the good news is that if you suck at fps games (like me), you can rely on your team's experienced players to carry you, or you can all just accept your fates and let the enemy kick your asses. The game is a lot of fun, and the characters are all unique and funny in their own way.

This is considered the father of hero shoters. You like a game where you can play with different characters? It probably got some inspiration from TF2. Other games like Overwatch, Paladins, Apex Legends and even LOL drank from the same source. As we all know, with great success comes great monetiza... Hphm... I mean, great memes and a great community. As expected, it happened to TF2 as well.
